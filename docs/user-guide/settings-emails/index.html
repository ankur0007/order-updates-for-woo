Settings — Emails

Quick-access list of every email this plugin sends, each linking through to the standard WooCommerce email customiser.

The Emails tab.

What this tab does

Every email the plugin sends — assignee notifications, @mention emails, customer notes, rating requests, rating follow-ups, deletion notices — is enumerated here with an enable/disable toggle. Click any email to deep-link into the matching WooCommerce email customiser for subject + body editing.

Available options

Assignee notification

Sent when a user is assigned an update or a customer rates an update they own.

@Mention notification

Sent when a user is @mentioned in an internal note. Bypasses per-update mutes.

Participant notification

Sent to participants when a new note is added to a thread they’ve participated in.

Customer notification

Sent to the customer when a customer-visible note is added (or on the “Notify customer” choice on solve/delete).

Customer rating request

Optional — sent to the customer after an update is solved, prompting them to rate.

Customer rating follow-up (promoter)

Sent to 4–5 star raters with share buttons.

Customer rating follow-up (detractor)

Sent to 1–3 star raters with an empathy message and a reply link.

Admin low-rating alert

Sent to the site admin email on detractor ratings.

Customer-update-deleted notice

Sent to the customer when an update they could see was deleted (only if “Notify” was chosen).

Creator-update-deleted notice

Sent to the staff member who created an update when another staff member deletes it.
